About BSU Safezone

BSU Safezone is a campus-wide safety and reporting platform created to help students submit concerns, emergency alerts, and incident reports easily and securely. Whether submitted anonymously or identified, every report is handled by campus authorities to maintain a safe and informed community.

Our mission is to build a safer, more aware, and more connected environment for all BSU students.

Safety Tips & Guidelines

Practical guides designed to help students stay prepared, aware, and confident during emergencies.

Personal Safety

Stay alert, use well-lit paths, avoid isolated areas, and report suspicious activity. Trust your instincts and prioritize your safety at all times.

First Aid Guides

Learn quick responses for cuts, burns, fainting, and sprains. Remember: ensure safety first and call for help if the situation becomes serious.

Fire Drills & Evacuation

Follow alarms calmly, use the nearest exit, avoid elevators, and proceed to your designated assembly area for headcount and instructions.
